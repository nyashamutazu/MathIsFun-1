Learn Basic Math Addition

Addition means joining things together. It can also be defined as Combining collections of objects together into a larger collection is Addition.It is represented by the ‘+’ sign. We can add the integers using the number line.

Simple Examples

1. Count 5 + 5 = ?

Explanation

5 + 5

Take 5 and then add 5 to it.

= 10
2. What is 8 + 6 = ?

Explanation

8 + 6

We will count 6 after 8. So we will get 14.

= 14
3. Lisa has 6 pencils and she buys 7 pencils, how many pencils does she have now?

Explanation

Lisa has 6 pencils.

She buys 7 pencils

So thats, 7 add 6

= 13

Word Examples

1. Matt has 5 marbles. Elizabeth gives him 9 more. How many marbles does Matt have now?

Explanation

Matt had 5 marbles. Elizabeth gave him more 9 marbles.

So thats 9 added 5.

5 + 9

= 14
2. A library has 100 Maths books, 50 English books and 230 Science books. How many books does the library book have all together.

Explanation

100 + 50 + 230

= 380
3. A school has 340 boys and 250 girls. How many children are there in the school?

Explanation

340 + 250

= 590

Carry Over Examples

1. What is 25 + 9?

Explanation

25 + 9

= 34
2. What is 599 + 366?

Explanation

599 + 366

3. John made 216 runs in one innings and 85 in the second. What was his total score for the two innings?
