Learn Basic Math Subtraction

Subtraction means taking things away from a group. We can subtract the integers using the number line. Add a positive integer by moving to the right on number line Or when we take away a given number of objects from a given collection of objects is Subtraction. It is represented by the ‘- ’ sign. We cannot subtract greater number from smaller number.

Simple Examples

1. Count 8 apples minus 2 apples = ?

Explanation

8 - 2

8 apples minus 2 apples leave 6 apples.

= 6
2. Subtract 5 from 589?

Explanation

589 - 5

Arrange the subtraction vertically. First subtract 5 units from 9 units.

Write 4 in the units place of difference. Write 8 in the tens place and 5 in the hundreds place.

= 584
3. Subtract 35 from 879?

Explanation

879 - 35

Arrange the subtraction vertically. First subtract 5 units from 9 units.

Write 4 in the units place of difference. Subtract 3 tens from 7 tens.

Write 4 in the tens place and 8 in the hundreds place.

= 844

Word Examples

1. Matt loses 9 marbles. Matt originally has 258 marbles. How many marbles does Matt have now?

Explanation

258 - 9

Arrange the subtraction vertically. We cannot subtract 9 units from 8 units.

Strike out the 5 in the tens place. Write 4 above it. Strike out the 8 in the unit place. Write 18 above it.

Write 9 in the units place in the difference. Write 4 in the tens place and 2 in the hundreds place.

= 249
2. A library has 786 books. Today 497 books were checked out of the library. How many books are left.

Explanation

786 - 497

= 289

Carry Over Examples

1. Sophia got 900 marks in the final exam. Lisa got 736 marks in the final exam. How many more marks did Sophia get than Lisa?

Explanation

900 - 736

= 164
